Questions, answered.

How does Cairn know what we worked on?

It connects to your version control and issue trackers with read-only access, then groups each person's merged pull requests, commits, and closed issues into a plain summary.

Do you store our source code?

No. Cairn reads metadata such as titles, authors, and timestamps. Your code never leaves your provider, and you can revoke access in one click.

Which tools do you integrate with?

GitHub, GitLab, Linear, Jira, and Bitbucket on the read side. Slack, email, and Notion for delivery. More are added every month.

Can we edit a summary before it goes out?

Yes. You can set a short review window so the digest waits for a quick edit, or let it post automatically. Your call, per channel.

Is there really a free plan?

Yes. Solo is free forever for one repository with a daily email summary. No card required to start.

How long does setup take?

A few minutes. Connect a provider, pick a channel and a time, and the first digest goes out the next working morning.
