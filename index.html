Questions, answered.

Do I need to know how to code?

If you can edit text in a file, you can use Pagewright. It is plain HTML, so you change the words directly, or ask an AI assistant to do it. No framework, no build tools, no terminal required.

How is this different from a free template?

Free templates give you an empty layout. Pagewright gives you the section order that converts and the words to fill it, with a written explanation of why each part works. That is the expensive part, and it is done.

Can I use it for client work?

Yes. Use it on unlimited personal and paid client projects. The only thing you cannot do is resell the templates themselves as templates.

Where can I host it?
