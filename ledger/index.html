Questions, answered.

How accurate is the counting?

Every event you send carries an idempotency key, so retries and at-least-once delivery never double count. The number on the invoice is the number you can reproduce from the audit trail.

Will recording usage slow down my API?

No. The SDK buffers and sends usage asynchronously, so a record call returns in microseconds and never sits in your request path.

Can I keep using Stripe?

Yes. Tollgate does the metering and the math, then pushes finished invoices into Stripe for collection. You keep your existing payments setup.

How do I migrate existing customers?

Import customers and current plans from a CSV or the API, then start sending events. You can run Tollgate in shadow mode next to your old system until the numbers match.

Is my usage data secure?

Events are encrypted in transit and at rest. Enterprise plans add SSO, SOC 2 reports, and a choice of data region.
