Questions, answered.

Is Fern really free?

Yes. You can track up to five plants, with identification, schedules, and reminders, free forever. Plus unlocks unlimited plants and the diagnosis tools.

Will it work for my weird plant?

Probably. Fern recognizes over 17,000 species, from common pothos to the rare aroid you overpaid for. If it is unsure, you can set the care details yourself.

Will it spam me with notifications?

No. One reminder on the day a plant needs care, and that is it. You can batch them to a single daily time if you prefer.

Is it on Android?

Yes, iOS and Android, with the same features on both. Your shelf syncs if you switch.

Do my photos stay private?

Yes. Photos are used to identify and diagnose, never sold or shared. You can delete them any time.
