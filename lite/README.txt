PAGEWRIGHT LITE (free)
======================

This is the free version of the Pagewright "Aurora" landing page template.
It is the full page: open index.html, change the words, change one color
variable in assets/css/styles.css, and deploy it anywhere for free.

THE ONE CONDITION
Keep the small "Built with Pagewright" credit in the footer. That is the deal
for the free version: use it for anything, just leave the credit.

WANT TO REMOVE THE CREDIT, OR GET MORE?
Pagewright Pro is a one-time purchase and adds:
  - The right to remove the credit, and a commercial license for client work.
  - ANNOTATIONS: a written guide to WHY each section is built to convert, so
    you can rewrite the copy without breaking what works.
  - A fill-in-the-blanks copywriting worksheet.
  - A copy-paste SEO + social meta tags file.
  - An optional one-command rebuild setup.
  - More templates, added over time.

Get Pro:  [your Gumroad / Lemon Squeezy link here]

HOW TO USE
  1. Open index.html in your browser.
  2. Edit the text to match your product.
  3. Recolor: edit the variables in assets/css/styles.css (:root and .dark).
  4. Deploy free: drag this folder onto https://app.netlify.com/drop
